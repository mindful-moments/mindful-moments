Frequently asked questions

Is Mindful Moments free?

Yes — the core app is free to download and use. There are no hidden charges or subscriptions required to access the guided meditations, journal, or progress tracking.

Does the app work without an internet connection?

Absolutely. Once installed, Mindful Moments works fully offline. All your sessions, journal entries, and progress are stored securely on your device.

What iOS version is required?

Mindful Moments requires iOS 17 or later and runs on all modern iPhones and iPads.

How do daily reminders work?

Head to the Settings tab in the app, choose a time that suits you, and turn on reminders. You'll need to allow notification permission when prompted — this is the only permission the app requests.

Can I delete my data?

Yes. Because all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by removing the app. Nothing is stored in the cloud or on external servers.
